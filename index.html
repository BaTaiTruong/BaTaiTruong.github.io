Hello and welcome to my website

I am a computational materials scientist working at the intersection of physics-based modeling and machine learning. My research focuses on understanding and designing materials through atomistic simulations, including density functional theory (DFT), molecular dynamics, and data-driven approaches. This website serves as a platform to share my research activities, tutorials, and open-source code. Here you will find practical guides on electronic structure calculations, machine learning for materials science, scientific programming, and data-driven materials discovery. Whether you are a student, researcher, or simply interested in computational materials science, I hope the resources provided here will be useful for your learning and research.

Thank you for visiting, and I invite you to explore the content and follow future updates.
